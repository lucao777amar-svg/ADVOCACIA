Who We Are

A Legacy of Legal Excellence

Founded in the heart of New York City by partners Edward Harrington and Margaret Cole, our firm was built on the conviction that every client — regardless of the complexity of their matter — deserves counsel of the highest caliber. What began as a boutique litigation practice has evolved into a full-service law firm with offices across three states and a reputation that speaks for itself.

Our approach is simple: we listen carefully, strategize deliberately, and advocate fiercely. From boardroom negotiations to courtroom battles, Harrington & Cole brings the same standard of excellence to every engagement.
